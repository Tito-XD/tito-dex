# PKHeX-derived encounter overlay

The normalized modern-game encounter overlays in this directory are generated
from [PKHeX](https://github.com/kwsch/PKHeX) commit
`5c9e949c9f0fa932a1b63511b32c2bee5ce75b4e`.

PKHeX is licensed under GPL-3.0-or-later. The generated overlay data and its
generator are distributed under the same license terms. TitoDex does not embed
or execute the PKHeX program in the app; only normalized facts and source
attribution are included in the offline/CDN data bundle.

The generator deliberately excludes trade arrays, gift arrays, egg encounters,
and static rows with a fixed acquisition ball. It imports wild slots, catchable
fixed encounters, raids, outbreaks, alpha Pokémon, and former titans.
